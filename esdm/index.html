ESDM Therapists

This is a web app to help parents and medical professionals find ESDM therapists in their area. It is based on the therapist list on the ESDM website. ESDM is one of the most powerful, evidence-based treatment modalities for children on the autistic spectrum. It uses playful socialization to teach children the communication skills and social habits that will help them to lead productive, fulfilling lives.
